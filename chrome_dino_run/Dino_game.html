T-Rex Run

This here is a variant of the Chrome Dinosaur game, which I tried to somewhat replicate. Sprites are courtesy of the internet and a little photo editing in GIMP. This game is not supported by Internet Explorer, or phones/tablets.Press the spacebar to jump over the Cacti. It takes "surviving" 4 cacti to go to the next level, with a cactus added onto this amount every 2 levels. The cacti will go by faster, so to make it slightly easier, expect a sharp increase in jump speed at levels 10 and 20. You will most likely lose, the question is how long you will last. Here is the link back to website you most likely came from. In the event that you press the spacebar and nothing happens, this means that not all images loaded. Refresh the page and try again. After a repeated number of attempts, the images will eventually all load. Enjoy!

This game doess not support the use of Internet Explorer.
